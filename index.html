modern

Modern dance is a broad genre of western concert or theatrical dance, primarily arising out of Germany and the United States in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.

ballet

Ballet is a type of performance dance that originated during the Italian Renaissance in the fifteenth century. It has since become a widespread, highly technical form of dance with its own vocabulary. It has been globally influential and has defined the foundational techniques used in many other dance genres and cultures.
